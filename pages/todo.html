Things to Do

Our favorite spots and experiences around Charm City.

Eat & Drink

R. House

301 West 29th Street

Our first date spot! R. House is a food hall featuring 10 counters of different cuisines. We had sushiritos-because it was 2017.

Tavern at Woodberry Kitchen

2010 Clipper Park Road

One of our personal favorites. Upscale New American fare.

The Charmery

801 West 36th Street

Ice cream so good that we bought merch.

Ministry of Brewing

1900 East Lombard Street

There are a ton of breweries all around Baltimore. This one is great!

Vaccaro's Italian Pastry Shop

222 Albemarle Street

Love this place! We've walked here in the pouring rain just for a slice of cake.

Experiences

Baltimore Museum of Art

10 Art Museum Drive

Visit the venue! A selection of galleries will be open during our wedding. To experience the full collection—including works by Henri Matisse, Pablo Picasso, Paul Cézanne, Edgar Degas, Paul Gauguin, Vincent van Gogh, and Pierre-Auguste Renoir—we recommend stopping by either before the wedding or on Sunday. You’ll also find plenty of fun, interactive exhibits to explore throughout the museum.

George Peabody Library

17 East Mount Vernon Place

Visit the library widely recognized as one of the most beautiful in the world. If you're lucky there will musicians from the conservatory playing throughout the building.

Johns Hopkins Homewood

3101 Wyman Park Drive

Walk around beautiful Homewood campus to see where we spent all of our time.
