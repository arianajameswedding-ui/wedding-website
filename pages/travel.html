Getting There

Travel & Stay

Everything you need for a seamless stay.

Train

Baltimore Penn Station is in the center of the city. In you're traveling from the New York area, we recommend taking Amtrack from either New York Penn or Newark Penn Station.

From Baltimore Penn, take a ride share to your hotel.

Plane

Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport (BWI) is the closest airport.

From BWI,take a ride share to your hotel.
