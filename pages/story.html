Our Story

A love story written across cities, seasons, and one magical proposal on the shores of Lake Como.

10 Years In The Making

Lake Como, March 2025

Our Timeline
2017

The Beginning

We were set up by a mutual friend and spent a few days texting before meeting in person. Somewhere between the messages and the first date, James promised he was “much funnier in person” — thankfully, he delivered.

2019

The College Years

We spent the next two and a half years of college making memories and exploring Baltimore together — supporting each other through long study nights, questionable cooking experiments, and everything in between.

2022

Early 20s

After a year spent mostly indoors, we finally hit the road for our first trips together — exploring small towns and coastal spots, always looking for an adventure.

2023

Growing our Family

In 2023, Chester entered our lives and quickly became the center of everything we do — for better or worse. From then on, every plan, trip, and quiet night at home has somehow revolved around him.

March 2025

The Proposal

We took our now-annual “big ski trip” to Zermatt, and Ari was convinced the proposal was coming. But as the two-week trip started winding down with no sign of it happening, Ari gave up on the idea. On the very last day, we took a day trip to Lake Como. James, clearly nervous, insisted we walk along the promenade before exploring the town. Ari became suspicious when he became very distracted, frantically searching for the photographer after accidentally going to the wrong meeting spot. When he got down on one knee, Ari — after being so certain for most of the trip — was stunned into silence. The rest of the day was spent celebrating across two Italian cities, drinking three bottles of champagne and soaking in every second of it.

June 2027

The Next Chapter

We’re so excited for this next chapter and so grateful for the love and support we’ve received over the last 10 years. It has meant the world to us to have our favorite people alongside us through every stage of our story.
